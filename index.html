About me

Programming (particularly web development) is a topic that has held my interest for years. I don't have an end goal in mind with these projects. I plan to just start off enjoying it as a hobby and seeing where that takes me. My portfolio is laughably small at this point, but creating the portfolio page now seems like it can be good motivator to fill it out as I create more. It will be cool to learn more about what it is I'm looking at when I come across web pages I find fascinating. Feedback will always be accepted - it's been the best way for me to learn new skills. I like to do things poorly and listen to those who know more.
