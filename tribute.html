Kristaps Porzingis

Some Notes on 3-6 Latvia

*Not everything may be true.

  • 4th overall pick (2015 - best value pick of all time)
  • 7 ft 3 in tall (2.21 m - for most of the world)
  • Power Forward and Center (dare I say Small Forward? Best ever at all 3?)

Kristaps Porzingis is the undisputed future winner of 10+ NBA Finals and regular season MVPs. Most experts agree he will be the first player to lead a team to a regular season 82-0 record. Knicks fans seem to hold a universal opinion that he will be joining Wilt in the 100 point club in the next year or two. Greatness like this hasn't been witnessed since Jordan himself (a conservative comparison). It's no surprise I thought of KP when the FreeCodeCamp's first project requested a tribute page.

This is the best big man to win the NBA All Star Weekend's Skills Challenge since Karl Anth(KAT)ony Towns. Oklahoma City's own Mr. "My Next Chapter" referred to this basketball diety as a unicorn (I really almost used 'an unicorn' here). I could go on all day about Zinger, but this is a coding project. I don't want to hype up a man who is the greatest at being humble.
